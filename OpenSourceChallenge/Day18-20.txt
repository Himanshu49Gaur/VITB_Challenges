Days 18-20: Advanced Contribution Practice & Profile Building

Continue searching for contributions, aiming for projects that genuinely interest you.
Consider contributing to projects you use regularly.
Respond to comments and feedback on your existing Pull Requests. Learn from any requested changes.
Refine your commit messages and PR descriptions for clarity and professionalism.
By the end of Day 20, you should have several contributions (Pull Requests) submitted, with some potentially merged.
