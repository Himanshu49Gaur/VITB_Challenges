Day 21: Review and Plan

Review your GitHub profile. See all the contributions you've made.
Reflect on what you've learned about Git, GitHub, and open source collaboration.
Identify areas where you feel confident and areas where you still want to improve.
Plan your next steps:
Continue contributing to the projects you've started.
Explore new projects.
Consider creating your own open-source project.
Learn more advanced Git concepts (rebase, cherry-pick, stashing).
